PowerUps

A parody of video games' most iconic character, Mario. Two players duke it out using various items referencing the Mario and Nintendo universe. Ignore the cabinet controls page. Player(1) Controls are 'WASD' and Space Bar. Player(2) Controls are Arrow Keys and Enter. Note: Don't be afraid to fall off.
