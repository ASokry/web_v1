Against the Wall

The first game I ever made. I wanted this game to be active in the sense that it kept players aware and on their toes while playing. As such, the game falls under the genre of "bullet hell". Controls: arrow keys/WASD and space bar. Also free to play at Sjsu Game Dev.

Download(For Windows)

Mashup

A parody of Jurrasic World. As some may know, vicious Raptors are finally tamed in the movie, Jurrasic World. To me I thought that was very amazing and an interesting take on the Jurassic Park series. I imagined what would it be like if other uncontrollable creatures of horror were finally tamed. What happens when humans finally tame alien xenomorphs?

Lie to Me

A re-design of the name and cover of the popular Pokemon franchise. It is a common tradition of pokemon to follow specific themes. However, since Pokemon is relatively "family friendly", mature themes are normally rejected. This project is to act as an opposite of this tradition, for it follows the theme of hell. Read the backside of the cover for small jokes and references, including the warning and publication.

Stay Dead

Most games require players to keep their health bar safe and well to stay alive. This game does not follow this basic rule. Instead, players will be trying to keep their health bar as low as possible or unwell in order to stay dead.

Download(Windows Only)
